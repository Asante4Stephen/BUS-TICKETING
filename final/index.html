STC Transportation

STC Transportation, a leading player in Ghana's transport sector, is synonymous with reliability and affordability. With an extensive network of routes, STC ensures competitive fares for various destinations, offering a cost-effective travel solution. Boasting well-maintained buses and a commitment to service excellence, STC stands out as a trusted choice for both short commutes and longer journeys. Choose STC for a comfortable and dependable journey across Ghana.

VIP Transportation

VIP Jeoun Transport is amongst the leading transportation companies in Ghana. Arguably one could also say it is the most competitive transport company in Ghana. It's quite not surprising therefore that many would want to know the fares and destinations of VIP buses.

2M Transportation

2M Express is the most reliable transport service provider in Ghana today with a solid driving record of 5 years and 99.9999% safety ranking. With about 130 employees (mostly drivers), 2M transports about 1,500 people daily. The company has a strict recruitment processes and hires the best drivers at all times.
